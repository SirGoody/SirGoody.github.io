Welcome

As you can tell, my name is Chase Wayne Goodman. I am currently a Computer Science major at Ball State University. The thing I like most about my area of study is the puzzle we get everyday. Figuring out each problem, or how to approach it, is always what I strive for. Branching off of that, that type of attitude makes me really observant and analytical. If I am to figure something out, I can't let something by. Whether it be by myself of with a group. I can work well in either situation. I played in 5 different varsity sports at Goshen High School so I am really good at working with a team and communicating amongst a group. I feel like I thrive in communication and teamwork. I am not afraid to speak up if I believe something is wrong or could have improvements. I believe if you have a group, why not get everyone involved and get them all thinking by bringing up questions about what we are doing. Bouncing ideas off of a group is the best way to get a product that everyone can be pleased about and feel like they have done their part.
