Enough about my desktop computers and my gaming outlets. Now it is time to tell you what kind of gamer I am. Honestly I like any kind of game. I will quickly name off some games in different genres that I love. Witcher 3, League of Legends, Battlefield 1, Stronghold, Civilization 6, Halo 3, and I can't really think of any other genres. My personal favorite game of all time is Witcher 3. Recently that game has become the most awarded game in history, and it definitely deserves it. The storyline is amazing, the graphics are the best I have seen for an open-world RPG, and the plot/genre makes the game more immersive. I honestly like the fact that the main character is set in stone as who he is, instead of like Skyrim. Because it is hard to base lore off of a random person that could be any gender and any race. Geralt of Rivia is by far my favorite game character now, and I have played all the other Witcher games because of the 3rd one. Other series I really enjoy is the Elder Scrolls series, and the Mass Effect series. I suppose you could say Open-world RPGs are my favorite type of games.
