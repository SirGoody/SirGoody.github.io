Gaming Life

Hello everyone, as some of you may know already, I run a YouTube channel called White Wolf Gaming. For me this is a fun way to relieve stress, gaming and uploading my videos to a community of great supporters. Thanks for my following I am rebranding my channel (previously called MrChexMateGaming) and professional getting a new logo for everything (twitter, twitch, youtube, facebook, and this website). I am first, and foremost, a PC gamer. Simply because computers could be used more for just gaming, plus what geek doesn't love a souped up gaming PC. My specs are NZXT 630 Black Windowed Edition case, MSI z87 G-45 Gaming motherboard, Intel Core i7 4770k, 32GB 2400MHz DDR3 RAM, MSI GTX 1070 Gaming X, 128 GB SSD (OS Drive), 2 TB HDD storage, Blu-ray read/write DVD drive, NZXT Kraken x61 liquid cooled, and a ton of aftermarket Bitfenix fans. I also have a desk that I custom painted myself with paint from Home Depot. I also have a second Desktop computer that I use for linux purposes. That is an Dell Inspiron 530 (2009) that I beefed up. It has a Intel Core 2 Quad Q9650, 8GB DDR2 800MHz, 500GB HDD, and a DVD drive. In that computer that is really all of the information that matters. I don't game on it, just do some web server stuff and terminal bashing.
